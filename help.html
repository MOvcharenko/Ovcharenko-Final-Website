Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Orders & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Can I refund or cancel my order?

Refunds are available for eligible purchases within 14 days, provided the game has not been downloaded or played.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! You can purchase digital gift cards in various denominations to share the gaming experience with friends.

2. Game Library & Downloads

How do I access my purchased games?

After purchase, your game will be available in your account library. You can download it anytime.

Do you offer DRM-free games?

Some games in our catalog are DRM-free. Look for the DRM-free label on the game page.

Can I re-download a purchased game?

Yes, any game you own can be re-downloaded at any time from your library.

3. Account & Security

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it via email.

Can I change the email linked to my account?

Yes, go to Account Settings > Email Preferences to update your email.

How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?

You can enable 2FA in your security settings for extra protection on your account.

4. Technical Issues & Support

My game isn’t launching. What should I do?

Ensure your system meets the game’s requirements, update your drivers, and restart your PC. If the issue persists, contact support.

The download is slow or stuck. What can I do?

Check your internet connection, restart the launcher, or try pausing and resuming the download.
